About EmberSky

One person. Infinite chaos. Pretending to be a studio.

Hey there. I’m the solo developer behind EmberSky.

Despite the fancy name and possibly professional vibes, it’s literally just me. I design, code, break things, fix them, and occasionally pretend there’s a team. There isn't. Unless you count coffee and spontaneous existential crises.

My goal? Build cool stuff, learn even cooler stuff, and maybe, just maybe, inspire a few others to do the same.

Whether it's game prototypes, web experiments, or interactive silliness — EmberSky is a digital playground that evolves as I do.
